Fuzzy Navel

Description

A classic cocktail from the 80's that's light and fruity. Easy to drink, this will be a hit at your next party!


Ingredients

  • 1 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Peach Schnapps
  • 4 oz Orange Juice

Steps

  1. Add ice to glass.
  2. Pour vodka, peach schnapps & orange juice into glass.
  3. Stir & Enjoy!
